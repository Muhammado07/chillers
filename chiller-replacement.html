The average life-span of chillers is 20-25 years, depending on type and operational load. And while preventive maintenance helps to maintain units in efficient operational condition, avoid high electricity bills and ensure reliable system operation; when the chiller’s operation in a facility exceed 10-12 years, the power consumption and maintenance cost increase drastically; while the operational efficiency decreases. So, it becomes necessary to replace them with new chillers, featuring latest VSD Technology and advanced specifications; that Save Up to 40-45% Energy Consumption; thus a replacement is definitely a smart move to save asset and running costs.
